Darth Vader

Discovered as a slave on Tatooine by Qui-Gon Jinn and Obi-Wan Kenobi, Anakin Skywalker had the potential to become one of the most powerful Jedi ever, and was believed by some to be the prophesied Chosen One who would bring balance to the Force.

Darth Maul

Darth Maul was an Iridonian (or Zabrak), born on Iridonia. At an early age he was trained as a Jedi, but was kidnapped by Darth Sidious, who conditioned him to forget everything he had learned from the Jedi. He then began to teach him the ways of the Dark Side.

Lord Sidious

Scheming, powerful, and evil to the core, Darth Sidious restored the Sith and destroyed the Jedi Order. Living a double life, Sidious was in fact Palpatine, a Naboo Senator and phantom menace.

Darth Bane

An ancient and legendary Sith Lord, it was Darth Bane who saw that the Sith traditions of old were ultimately a dead end. All too often, squabbling Sith in their bid for power upended carefully laid plans.

Darth Revan

Revan is a fictional character in the Star Wars franchise, created for BioWare's 2003 role-playing video game Star Wars: Knights of the Old Republic.
